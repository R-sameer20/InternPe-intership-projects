Who We Are

Who We Are?

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Nagpur, Maharashtra, Vastra was born from a simple conviction: that India's rich textile heritage deserves a modern, premium platform. Our founders — a team of designers, weavers, and entrepreneurs from Vidarbha — set out to bridge the gap between India's artisan clusters and its growing fashion-forward urban consumer.

Today, Vastra is home to three distinct labels — Vastra, Nirmaan, and Kala Collective — each with a unique design philosophy rooted in regional craft traditions: Paithani weaves from Aurangabad, Chanderi silks from Madhya Pradesh, and Ajrakh block prints from Gujarat and Rajasthan.

Our Nagpur headquarters serves as both our design studio and sourcing hub, keeping us close to the artisan communities we champion. Every garment carries a story — of hands that wove it, villages that dyed it, and generations that perfected it.

Our Values

Craft, Community & Commerce

We operate on a Fair Trade sourcing model, guaranteeing living wages and safe working conditions to every artisan partner in our network. Over 600 weaver families across Maharashtra, Rajasthan, and Gujarat depend on Vastra's supply chain as a stable income source.

Sustainability isn't a marketing checkbox for us — it's baked into our production process. We use GOTS-certified organic cotton, AZO-free natural dyes, and minimal-waste pattern-cutting techniques developed with the National Institute of Fashion Technology, Mumbai.

Our Nagpur head office runs on 80% solar energy. By 2027, we aim to make all our packaging 100% compostable and achieve net-zero carbon across our logistics chain.
